Colorful Pencil Holder

Difficulty: Intermediate
Time: 20-30 minutes

Materials You'll Need

Tin Can

Acts as the base for the organizer.

Colorful Wooden Sticks

Decorate the outer surface with vibrant colors.

Double Tape

Used to stick the wooden sticks to the can.

Scissors

To cut the tape to the needed size.

Step-by-Step Guide

1

Prepare the Tin Can

Clean and dry the tin can before decorating.

2

Trim the Sticks

Use scissors to cut each wooden stick to match the height of the tin can.

3

Apply Tape to Each Stick

Stick a small piece of double tape on the back of each trimmed stick.

4

Stick the Wooden Sticks

Peel off the tape backing and press each stick vertically onto the can.

5

Final Adjustments

Make sure all sticks are aligned well and pressed firmly in place.

6

Use Your Organizer

Fill the organizer with pens, brushes, or other desk items.
